Privacy Policy — Image GPT

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately
Image GPT processes user-uploaded images for branding, resizing, and optimization. We do not sell or misuse your data, and your assets remain your property.

1. What We Are

Image GPT by Primacy Infotech is an AI-powered image processing tool designed to automatically resize, optimize, and apply branding elements (such as logos and layouts) to images for digital platforms including social media and marketing campaigns.

2. Information We Collect

  • Images uploaded by users for processing
  • Brand assets such as logos (if provided by the user)
  • Basic account information (email, name) for authentication
  • Usage metadata (processing requests, formats selected)

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as passwords, financial information, or private communications.

3. How We Use Data

  • To process and generate optimized branded images
  • To improve AI performance and output quality
  • To provide user-specific customization and presets

We do not sell or distribute user-uploaded images or assets to third parties.

4. Image Handling & Ownership

  • All uploaded images remain the intellectual property of the user
  • Images are processed temporarily and not used for public datasets
  • No images are shared externally without user consent

5. LinkedIn Authentication

If you choose to log in via LinkedIn OAuth, we access only:

  • r_liteprofile — basic profile (name, photo)
  • r_emailaddress — email for account creation

We do not access connections, messages, or additional LinkedIn data.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is transmitted securely using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Temporary image data is deleted after processing or within a short retention window
  • Secure access controls are enforced across all services

7. Data Retention

Uploaded images are stored only temporarily for processing purposes and are automatically deleted after completion or within 24–72 hours unless explicitly saved by the user.

8. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Request removal of stored assets
  • Access and review your data

9. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

10. Compliance

This service complies with applicable Indian IT laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and follows global best practices aligned with GDPR principles where applicable.
