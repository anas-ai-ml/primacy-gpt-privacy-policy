Data Harvester AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately
Data Harvester AI collects only publicly available business information. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data of individuals.

1. What We Are

Data Harvester AI is a B2B business intelligence tool that aggregates publicly available company information — such as company names, addresses, phone numbers, and industry classifications — from public web sources for legitimate business research purposes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the following types of publicly available business data:

  • Company names, websites, and registered addresses
  • Business phone numbers and general contact email addresses
  • Industry type, employee count ranges, and company size
  • Publicly listed director and executive names
  • Business registration information from public government registries

We do not collect passwords, financial credentials, private messages, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Collected Data

  • To provide B2B prospecting and market research capabilities
  • To help businesses identify potential vendors, partners, or clients
  • To compile business directories for internal use by subscribing organizations

We do not sell individual personal data to third parties.

4. LinkedIn Data

When users authenticate via LinkedIn OAuth, we access only the permissions explicitly granted:

  • r_liteprofile — basic profile (name, photo) for login only
  • r_emailaddress — email address for account creation only

We do not store LinkedIn profile data beyond what is necessary for authentication. We do not access your LinkedIn connections, messages, or any data beyond the granted scopes.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • We implement rate limiting and access controls on all stored data
  • We do not share data with third parties except as required by law
  • Users may request deletion of their account data at any time

6. Data Retention

Business contact data is retained for up to 90 days from collection, after which it is refreshed or removed. User account data is retained until account deletion is requested.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to data we hold about your organization
  • Request correction of inaccurate business information
  • Request removal of your business from our database
  • Opt out of data collection for your business

8. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Compliance

This tool is operated in compliance with applicable Indian IT laws, the Information Technology Act 2000, and applicable international data protection standards including GDPR principles for EU users.
